## Didaktischer Mix

- **70 % Anwendung / 30 % Theorie** (im 1-Tag-Format ist Tempo höher)
- **Live-Coding statt Folienschlachten** — jede Mechanik am offenen Editor
- **Eine Hands-on-Übung pro Modul**, am Ende eigenes Projekt
- **Begleitmaterial:** CLAUDE.md-Vorlagen, Skill-Beispiele, Cheat-Sheets, **17 Trainer-Demos** (Anhang 99.07), **41 SkillCards** (Anhang 99.08) — bleibt euch nach dem Workshop

- Modul 5 fokussiert auf die **wichtigsten 3–4 Anti-Patterns** + Security-Top-5; Detail-Liste im Anhang
- 2-Tage-Variante hat hier deutlich mehr Tiefe (Compliance-Checkliste, IP-Audit, Team-Konventionen)
</NoteCard>

<En>
| # | Module | 1-day duration | Outcome |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Foundations & Mindset** | 30 min | Position augmented working, justify your tool choice |
| 2 | **Setup & Configuration** | 60 min | Claude Code running locally + first `CLAUDE.md` |
| 3 | **Skills, Subagents & MCP** | 90 min | One own skill in production, MCP server wired in |
| 4 | **Spec-Driven Development** | 75 min | Spec → plan → code as a repeatable workflow |
| 5 | **Best Practices, Anti-Patterns** | 45 min | Top anti-patterns + security rules of thumb |
| 6 | **Hands-on: Your projects** | 75 min | First result in your own repo |

## Didactic mix

- **70 % practice / 30 % theory** (1-day format runs at higher tempo)
- **Live coding, not slide shows** — every mechanic shown in the editor
- **One hands-on per module**, then your own project at the end
- **Companion material:** CLAUDE.md templates, skill examples, cheat sheets,
  **17 trainer demos** (appendix 99.07), **41 SkillCards** (appendix 99.08) —
  yours to keep after the workshop

<NoteCard variant="hint" title="What we keep short / skip">
- Modules 1 + 2 condensed — setup details are in the pre-workshop email, only live demo here
- Module 5 focuses on the **top 3–4 anti-patterns** + security top 5; deep list in appendix
- 2-day intensive goes much deeper here (compliance checklist, IP audit, team conventions)
</NoteCard>
</En>
