# <I18n de="Willkommen" en="Welcome" />

<De>
## Worum es heute geht

- Wir machen euch produktiv mit **Claude Code** — nicht „klick mal hier, klick mal da".
- Ihr lernt, wann KI delegiert wird — und wann **nicht**.
- Am Ende des Workshops habt ihr ein **konkretes Ergebnis im eigenen Repo**.

| Block | Inhalt | Dauer |

<En>
## What today is about

- We make you productive with **Claude Code** — not "click here, click there".
- You learn when to delegate to AI — and when **not**.
- By the end of the workshop you ship **a concrete result in your own repo**.

## Day structure (intensive format, 2 days)

| Block | Content | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| Day 1 morning | Module 1 + 2 — mindset, setup | 4 hrs |
| Day 1 afternoon | Module 3 — skills, subagents, MCP | 4 hrs |
| Day 2 morning | Module 4 + 5 — spec-driven, best practices | 4 hrs |
| Day 2 afternoon | Module 6 — your own projects | 4 hrs |

## Ground rules

- **Ask anytime** — we're not a conference, we're a workshop.
- **Coding along is mandatory** — listening alone won't cut it.
- **No phone multitasking** — AI doesn't replace attention.
- **What happens here stays here** — code, data, discussions.
</En>
